ESG Report

An ESG report is a document that companies publish to detail their environmental, social, and governance (ESG) performance. It is a way for companies to share their sustainability efforts with stakeholders, including investors, customers, and employees. The report includes information on the company's impact on the environment, its social initiatives, and its governance practices. ESG reports are becoming increasingly important for companies as consumers and investors are paying more attention to sustainability and ethical practices.
